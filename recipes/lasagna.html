Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, a splash of red wine, or a pinch of sugar

Steps

Follow these instructions to prepare your lasagna:
  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. (Optional) Make the béchamel sauce
  5. Assemble the lasagna
  6. Bake
  7. Cool and serve
